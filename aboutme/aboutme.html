AMIR EID

Amir Eid is an architect, multi-disciplinary artist, marketer, and the founder & CEO of Visionario Play.
He believes that diverse work experiences are essential for personal and professional growth in today’s world, fueling creativity with a blend of knowledge and skills.
Eid has collaborated on various projects and with notable organizations including EconoWin-GIZ Egypt,
the Cairo Institute for Human Rights Studies, the Cairo Contemporary Dance Center, Bashkatib, and the Regional Center for Rights and Liberties.
He was awarded a fellowship with Institut für Auslandsbeziehungen in Germany, gaining experience with Spielmaterial.
Expanding into sports photography and videography, he worked with Water Proof World, further diversifying his creative portfolio.
In 2021, Eid founded Visionario Play, one of the few entities producing board and card games in Egypt.
He also launched personal artistic projects, including a documentary series on Mamlouk Minbars in Cairo, which was screened at the Victoria and Albert Museum in London.
Currently based in Florence, Eid is advancing his programming career at 42 Florence while continuing to pursue his artistic passions.

  • Location: Florence, Italy
  • Busy with: Full-time Student at 42 Firenze
